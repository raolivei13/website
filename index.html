ECE PhD student at UC San Diego.

I am a first year Electrical and Computer Engineering PhD student at University of California, San Diego Jacobs School of Engineering. I am interested in the theoretical foundations of artificial intelligence, with research interests at the intersection of optimization theory, compressed sensing, and theoretical neuroscience.

I received my MSc. in Electrical Engineering, Signal and Image Processing and my BSc. in Electrical Engineering, Machine Learning and Controls from the University of California, San Diego, La Jolla, USA.

Currently, I am working for a startup.
